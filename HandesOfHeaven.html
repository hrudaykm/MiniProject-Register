About the Event

Hands of Heaven celebrates the art of planning and executing extraordinary events. Participants step into the role of event planners, where they must strategize, coordinate resources, manage teams, and handle challenges to deliver a successful event. The competition tests creativity, leadership, adaptability, and problem- solving skills, reflecting the real-world dynamics of professional event management. Success lies in turning ideas into memorable experiences through teamwork, precision, and innovation.
