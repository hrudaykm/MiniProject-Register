About the Event

Just like Tatara Fujita from Welcome to the Ballroom, every dancer starts with passion, determination, and the courage to step onto the stage. Ballroom dance is not just about perfect steps-it is about rhythm, teamwork, confidence, and expressing emotion through movement. In the same way, this group dance competition celebrates the energy and dedication of every performer. Each team brings creativity, coordination, and powerful stage presence to deliver a performance that excites the audience and leaves a lasting impression. Let your passion guide your steps, synchronize with your team, and create a performance that shines with energy and unity.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Each team must have a minimum of 5 members and a maximum of 9 members.
  2. The total performance time is 5 (4+1) minutes including stage setup
  3. Only Hindi and Tamil songs are allowed for the mashup. Songs from other languages are not permitted. Audio must be clear. All audio tracks must be submitted one day before the event.
  4. Vulgar or inappropriate lyrics/content in the music and vulgar costumes are strictly prohibited. ( if you are confused contact the Cordinators)
  5. Judging based on stage usage, coordination and overall coordination
  6. Teams are allowed to use props/properties for their performance. However, the use of water, fire, or animals is strictly not allowed. If any of the above rules are violated, marks will be deducted from the total score.
  7. The decision of the judges will be final.
