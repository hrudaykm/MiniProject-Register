GENERAL RULES

* Each team may consist of Maximum 25 - 30, excluding the Group Dance event.

* Participants must confirm their participation on or before 02/04/2026.

* Participants are required to report to the registration desk by 8:30AM.

* Participants should carry their own laptop, dongle, flash drive, mobile,headset, etc.

* College Identity Card must be produced at the registration counter.

* The Registration fee Rs.2000 per team (inclusive of all events except group dance ), registration fee for Group Dance is ₹500 per team.

* No refund will be provided once registration is completed.

* The Decision of Judges will be final and binding.

* Vulgarity and Obscenity are strictly prohibited.

* Teams will be considered for Overall Championship only if participated in all events.

* Group Dance will not be included in the Overall Championship.

* There is no limit on the number of teams a college can send for each event.

* Participants are required to register early, as there is a pre-night task in Event Management, Best Manager & The Entrepreneurs.
